# Hydrophobic Field Harvesters

*Passive Adsorbers for Waterborne Microplastics Removal*

## Overview

The Hydrophobic Field Harvesters (HFHs) are a passive, modular technology designed to extract hydrophobic contaminants from water sources, including:
- Microplastics
- Oils
- PFAS chemicals
- Synthetic runoff

## Product Series

### 1. HFH::FlowTrap Series
**Urban Runoff Interceptors**

**Purpose**: Capture pollutants at discharge points before entering natural waterways

**Deployment Points**:
- Washing machine lines
- Shower lines
- Storm drains
- Downspouts
- Greywater pipes
- Commercial floor drains

**Key Features**:
- Modular cartridges with layered hydrophobic sorbents
- DIY-install or wall-mount housing
- Built-in overflow safeguards
- No power required
- Optional saturation indicator

### 2. HFH::Field Series
**Distributed Environmental Harvesters**

**Purpose**: Passively adsorb floating microplastics and pollutants from open water environments

**Deployment Points**:
- Shorelines and docks
- Ponds
- Stormwater retention basins
- Lakefront areas
- Slow-moving river sections

**Key Features**:
- Compact floating design
- Exchangeable sorbent core
- No power or anchors required
- Optional solar-assisted active model
